The
COR-S
Project

A subpart of the COR project - A collaborative project between the Centre for Language Technology at UCPH, the Society for Danish Language and Literature (DSL), the Danish Language Council, and the Danish Agency for Digitisation.

The aim is to develop a Danish language resource of that contain the core information about the words (i.e., lemmas) of the language for AI purposes. 
The COR-S project focuses on making a coarse-grained sense inventory from current lexical resources (The Danish Dictionary and DanNet).

Master's Thesis
Project

An investigation of the optimal approach for creating Danish sense representations from a combination of word embedding models and handcrafted lexical resources. 

The aim of the sense representations is to use them for automatic sense tagging, which could enable other semantic tasks like sense clustering or sentiment analysis.
